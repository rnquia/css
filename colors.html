R A I N B O W COLORS






Red is the color of blood, so it has historically been associated with sacrifice, danger, and courage. It is also most commonly associated with heat, activity, passion, etc.
Orange convey a message of positivity, and that is why many brand use it to catch attention of the viewers.
Yellow, the color of the sun, brings positive feelings, happiness, and joy.
Green is a color associated mostly with nature and Earth, and it can represent beginning and growth.
Blue is a primary and calming color that often symbolizes stability, wisdom, and sadness.
Indigo is dark color between blue and violet spectrum. It represents tranquility, confidence, and integrity.
Violet is said to be a royal color and is associated with luxury, power, and ambition.
